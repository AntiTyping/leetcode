Accelerated Learning Plan

A 4-phase, ~7-week plan targeting the gaps above while preserving the daily-practice habit.

Phase 1 — Graph FoundationsWeeks 1-2

Close the biggest gap. Build BFS/DFS templates and apply them to grid and adjacency-list graphs.

  • #133 Clone Graph, #207 Course Schedule, #210 Course Schedule II (topological sort)
  • #127 Word Ladder (BFS shortest path), #743 Network Delay Time (Dijkstra)
  • Reinforce existing #200 Number of Islands and #797 All Paths with explicit BFS/DFS variants
Phase 2 — Union-Find & ConnectivityWeeks 3-4

Introduce the disjoint-set data structure (path compression + union by rank) and its canonical problems.

  • #547 Number of Provinces, #684 Redundant Connection
  • Re-solve #200 Number of Islands with union-find for contrast
  • #990 Satisfiability of Equality Equations (stretch)
Phase 3 — Stacks, Heaps & DesignWeeks 5-6

Build monotonic-stack intuition and tackle the marquee design problems interviewers love.

  • #146 LRU Cache, #155 Min Stack, #150 Evaluate Reverse Polish Notation
  • #84 Largest Rectangle in Histogram, revisit #739 Daily Temperatures with monotonic stack
  • #23 Merge K Sorted Lists (heap), #211 Design Add and Search Words (Trie)
Phase 4 — Trees & Bit ManipulationWeek 7

Round out tree traversal patterns and shore up the bit-manipulation blind spot.

  • #230 Kth Smallest in BST, #105 Construct Binary Tree, #236 LCA, #226 Invert Tree
  • #338 Counting Bits, #136 Single Number
  • #48 Rotate Image (matrix manipulation)
